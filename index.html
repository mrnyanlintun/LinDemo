Multi-Model Simulation Demo for Doctor of Engineering in Engineering Management Praxis by Nyan Lin Tun

Multi-model simulation prepared for Doctor of Engineering praxis in Engineering Management at The George Washington University, School of Engineering and Applied Science. This demo uses synthetic project folders and multi-model simulations to demonstrate multi-model capability of probabilistic EVM forecasting, anomaly detection, document-risk extraction, signal synthesis, and governance recommendation logic. It is not an official George Washington University system, not a commercial product, and not intended for public release, procurement, operations, or official project-control use.
