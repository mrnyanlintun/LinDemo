Reporting CycleEach refresh reads the current period from EVM, schedule, RFI, submittal, cost, pay-app, design-comment, and field records.Cycle t = new folder refresh + new project-control records
Monte Carlo EnginePV, EV, AC, CPI, SPI, and BAC feed uncertainty propagation for EAC and finish-slip percentiles.P50 / P80 = percentiles from simulated EAC and finish outcomes
Bayesian UpdateThe prior risk from the last cycle is updated using new likelihood evidence from EVM, anomaly, and document signals.Posterior odds = prior odds × likelihood ratio
SPC / CUSUMControl-limit and cumulative-drift rules detect whether CPI/SPI trends are moving out of expected range.CUSUM_t = max(0, CUSUM_{t-1} + drift - allowance)
Signal SynthesisThe status is based on forecast percentiles, posterior risk, anomaly flags, document risk, and conflict logic.Signal = Monte Carlo + Bayesian + SPC/CUSUM + DOCS
Governance GateGreen, Amber, or Red-review is not automatic direction. It remains advisory until the human authority confirms action.Recommendation = evidence package + authority threshold + audit trail
Multi-Model Simulation Demo for Doctor of Engineering in Engineering Management Praxis by Nyan Lin Tun
Multi-model simulation prepared for Doctor of Engineering praxis in Engineering Management at The George Washington University, School of Engineering and Applied Science. This demo uses synthetic project folders and multi-model simulations to demonstrate multi-model capability of hybrid Monte Carlo/Bayesian EVM forecasting, SPC/CUSUM anomaly simulation, document-risk extraction, signal synthesis, and governance recommendation logic. It is not an official George Washington University system, not a commercial product, and not intended for public release, procurement, operations, or official project-control use.
